Orwell tells us what is wrong. Forest tells us what to do about it.

In a world of surveillance and social credit they welcome us to tell us we do not belong. Fed endless waste priced ever higher, called free while they tell us what to do, think and say. Dissent is hate speech, a crime. To buy a house of pine and chalk enslaves us for life. We trade legacy for landfill, ruled 'for our own good' by systems that are 'fair' because they treat us all as criminals.

He walked away, to live in a forest. Others found solace there, and stayed. There was peace, then plenty, so civilisation tried to bring them down. They saw it coming, and wound the clock to midnight.

He failed on his own terms; queened a pawn and set her free, dooming her forever.
