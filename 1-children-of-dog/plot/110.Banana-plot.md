# Banana - Plot Summary

## Key Events

- Cattle distress from Macintyre place (south valley)
- **Banana**: 1700kg Brahman cross prize bull, surrounded by dogweeds
- Five antithesis already trampled/gored by bull
- Macintyre boys (Bryce 17, Cameron 19) shooting methodically
- Forest arrives with combat rifle, clears perimeter
- Bull standing still - Model Seven (zombie worm) inside him
- Forest injects anti-parasitic into bull's neck
- Banana bellows in rage, charges, collapses from side effects
- Neural damage during worm insertion attempt
- Mother cow claims Banana, grooms him
- Prognosis: motor function recovers in 48 hours, cognitive function unknown
- Won't know if breeding capability intact until tested
- Daniel Macintyre (father) arrives, grateful
- "Banana" named for shire (itself named for prize bull's impressive anatomy)
- Forest leaves anti-Seven pills for family and cattle
- Theme: Community defending each other's assets, uncertainty about recovery
