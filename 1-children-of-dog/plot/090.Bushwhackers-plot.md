# Bushwhackers - Plot Summary

## Key Events

- Trix sharpens knives, processing stress from previous night
- Forest demonstrates fleshmelters to her
- Citrus grove damaged by antithesis, Forest drags carcasses clear
- Janna arrives alive (Forest had assumed she was dead from seeing bones)
- Janna's nursery destroyed, facing bankruptcy
- Forest gives Janna Hummingbird and fleshmelter, 5-minute instruction
- Invites her to dinner: "you are not alone"
- Drive to Oleksiy's - finds him cleaning rifle, defence complete
- Check Gerry's place (away for two weeks)
- Oleksiy spots antithesis, Forest clears with Hummingbird
- Gets spiked in thigh during cleanup
- Autumn warns of Model Five in tree, they take cover
- Theme: Community checking on neighbors, arming them, practical competence
