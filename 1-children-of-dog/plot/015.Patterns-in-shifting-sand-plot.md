# Patterns in Shifting Sand - Plot Summary

## Key Events

### Trouble Brewing (Flashback)
- Age 12, mother brings home ginger beer bug
- Makes bootleg alcoholic ginger beer for fishermen
- Police catch him distributing, choose not to prosecute
- Officer: "This will not happen again"
- Learns: systems have gaps, ignorance no excuse, understanding gaps lets you work within them

### Horsing Around
- Two boys (tall and short) ride horses through city CBD
- Invoke Pastoral Accommodation Act of 1847 at police station
- Require water and hay for horses (two silver shillings payment)
- Young constable hostile, Senior Sergeant sees opportunity
- Calls newspaper, creates public relations triumph
- Horses watered at fountain in busiest intersection
- Sergeant lectures on history of the town and fountain
- Theme: Old laws still have power, pattern recognition beats systems
