# The Public Interest - Plot Summary

## Key Events

- Morning after hive destruction, Forest has many points
- Autumn wants to instrument valley with sensors, quantum-entangled tracking
- Convoy arrives: government, corporate, scientists
- **Sophia Hachia** (Project Liaison, platinum hair, ice queen bureaucrat)
- Scientists: Dr. Kellerman (xenobiology), Dr. Morrison (materials), Dr. Singh (environmental), Dr. Chen
- Corporate reps: Thompson (Austral Defense), Walsh (Advanced Materials), Park (tech)
- Forest offers Autumn as having "interns" - Chen bristles but accepts
- Trix works the crowd with tea, gathers intelligence
- Deal struck: research station on Gerry's property (center valley, good terrain)
- Dr. Singh most practical, focuses on logistics and safety
- Sophia observes, controls, takes notes
- Forest and Autumn plan: give scientists gated access to tech, control information flow
- Autumn: "You want me to play god." Forest: "I'm in."
- Four memorial stones mentioned (foreshadowing)
- Theme: Forest manipulates situation to control tech flow while appearing cooperative
